Ever Heard of Ratatouille?

A Provencal-style dish with an air of summer simplicity

Generous amounts of parmesean mixed with soft, fresh vegetables give this dish its iconic history

What You'll Need

  • 3 small tomatoes
  • 2 yellow squash (arge)
  • 1 large zucchini
  • 1 small eggplant
  • 1 tspn salt (Kosher, divided)
  • 6 tbspn extra-virgin olive oil
  • 1 grated garlic clove
  • 2 oz Parmesan cheese
